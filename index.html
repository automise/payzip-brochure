what is payzip?

PayZip is a club membership invoicing and payments system. It allows your members to pay by card whilst still capturing cash, cheque and bank payments.

Record all your club's payments quickly, and free-up rehearsal or training time by letting your members pay when it's convenient for them.

Safe & Secure

Card payments are securely processed by Barclaycard SmartPay. We group your online payments into one weekly payment into your club's account and give you a full breakdown, so you know exactly who's paid and for what.

Always Up-to-date

As a treasurer, it's vital to have up-to-date information on-hand. Our dashboard and reports allow you to quickly see what's been paid and what's outstanding. Online payments are shown immediately, so you're always up-to-date.

Easily Pay Online

Many club members would much prefer to pay their subs online - just ask them! Our invoices and payment page are designed for smartphones and tablets, so members can easily pay you wherever they are, whenever it suits them. They'll love you for it.

One-click Reminders

Does someone need a gentle payment reminder? PayZip has you covered. With just one click you can send a reminder to everyone who currently owes you money. No more manual emails, no awkward conversations.

Built for Change

We understand that treasurers can change from time-to-time. No problem. Just add the new treasurer as an additional club organiser, making all the club's historical data available to them. Your committee and auditors will thank you.

Quick & Simple

When making their first payment, members can choose to securely store their payment details with Barclaycard. The next time they pay, they just enter their card security code, so paying again takes a matter of seconds.
