How PayZip can save you time

We built PayZip with Treasurers in mind so we have included lots of features that will save you time.
Members
Invoices
Payments
Admin
Bulk import

Import your existing members in one go, using an Excel or CSV file.  It’s simple to add individual members as well.

Group

Place your members into one or more groups - e.g. 'Under 21s' and 'Summer Tour'. Whatever makes sense for your club.

No registration

Your members don’t need to register with PayZip to pay you, but if they do they’ll get their own personal dashboard.

Manage

Add, edit and archive members as you need. It’s straightforward to move members up to the next group too.

Customise recipients

Send your invoice to everyone or just one person. Pick whole groups or specific individuals - the choice is yours.

Re-use invoices

Save time creating new invoices - simply use a copy of an existing invoice as the template for your new one.

Simple invoicing

Customise the invoice name, description and additional line items for greater detail. You can even pick a due date.

Tailored invoices

PayZip makes it easy to customise specific invoices so you can charge certain members a different amount.

Multiple options

Members can pay by card, cash or cheque. Online payments are made securely via Barclaycard (Pro plan only).

Email receipts

PayZip sends email receipts to your members, no matter which method they used to pay as soon as payment is recorded.

One-click reminders

Give those members who haven’t paid yet a gentle nudge, using our one-click reminders making chasing a thing of the past.

Dashboard

Instantly see who has paid, and who still owes money to the club, broken down by group if you need to see more detail.

Weekly payments

Simplify your bank reconciliations. You'll receive member payments in one simple, weekly payment from us.

Remittances

With each payment, we'll email you a full breakdown of each payment - see who paid, how much, and for what.

Reports

Generate transaction reports,  member lists and outstanding payments - helping you prepare for committee meetings.

Administrators

Add multiple club administrators to help you organise the club’s finances and share the workload with others.
