Information and FAQs

What is PayZip?

PayZip is a payment mechanism allowing your members to pay their club and affiliation fees online, if they choose to do so. It integrates seamlessly with the EA Secretaries' Portal, allowing payments to be collected quickly and simply.

Safe & Secure

Card payments are securely processed by Barclaycard SmartPay. We group your online payments into one weekly payment into your club's account and give you a full breakdown, so you know exactly who's paid, and for what.

Always Up-to-date

As a treasurer, it's vital to have up-to-date information on-hand. Online payments are shown immediately, so you're always up-to-date.

Pay Online Easily

Our invoice and payment pages are designed for smartphones and tablets, so members can easily pay you wherever they are, whenever it suits them. They'll love you for it.

Frequently asked questions

EA Club Members

What is "PAYZIP PENARTH" and why is it on my card statement?
We help clubs and not-for-profit groups collect money from their members.  If you see PAYZIP PENARTH on your credit card or bank statement, it means you’ve recently paid a club or organisation a membership fee.  If you have any concerns about a transaction, please get in touch with us by emailing help@payzip.co.uk and we’ll be happy to provide further details.
Do I need to create an account with PayZip to pay my registration fees?
No. As an EA athlete, you won't create a PayZip account. Details of the athlete payments are stored in the EA Secretaries' Portal, so you won't be able to view them through the PayZip dashboard.
I have a query with my payment - who should I speak to?
In the first instance, speak with your club's membership secretary or treasurer. If they need the matter investigated further, they can contact the England Athletics support team. We can also refer you to them, so contact us at help@payzip.co.uk if you don't have their details.

EA Club Organisers

I want to take online payments with my club - how do I get started?
We have built a custom integration with the EA Secretaries’ Portal and all interactions are controlled through the Portal.  Do not create a club through the PayZip website - this will not link-up properly with England Athletics’ system and will not properly track your members’ registration payments in the way that you expect.  Additionally, using the Portal integration ensures you receive the special discounted service fee, which England Athletics has negotiated with us for its clubs.

Refer to England Athletics' Help & Support Section for further assistance.
How much is the PayZip service fee?
The England Athletics support team will be able to advise the discounted rate which EA clubs receive.
When will I receive payments from my club's members?
We pay you once a week on a Wednesday for all payments up to and including the previous Wednesday. We also send a remittance advice email to the secretary's email address. This has a Secretaries' Portal link in it, which shows the full breakdown of the money being paid to the club.
Can my members pay the EA Registration Fee this way?
Yes. The EA Portal tells PayZip what the money being collected is for. Where applicable, we separate out the EA Registration Fee for you and make sure this is paid to England Athletics. Any remaining money, less the service fee, is paid to the club.
I have a question that isn't answered here.
No problem, just get in touch with us at help@payzip.co.uk and we'll be happy to help.
